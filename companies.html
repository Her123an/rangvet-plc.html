Our Companies

Together, these companies form the RANGVet Group — offering solutions in agriculture, veterinary services, trade, engineering, and real estate.

1. RANGVET PLC

Core Focus: Import and distribution of veterinary drugs, agrochemicals, and vegetable seeds. Serves farmers, cooperatives, agro-dealers, and government institutions nationwide.

  • Offers technical services and product application training
  • Future-focused, with a vision to establish local manufacturing facilities
  • Committed to affordable, high-quality, and reliable agricultural solutions

2. GAWT International Business

A leading Ethiopian trader, importer, exporter, and supplier of agrochemicals, fertilizers, and seeds.

3. Rang Engineering

Focused on importing agricultural equipment and mechanization solutions, Rang Engineering supports Ethiopia's modern farming transformation by:

  • Providing durable and innovative machinery
  • Supporting farmers with installation, training, and after-sales services
  • Empowering productivity through modern tools

4. R.A.W.

R.A.W. is a dynamic import-export business based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, specializing in:

  • Exporting agricultural commodities from Ethiopia to international markets
  • Importing agrochemicals, vegetable seeds, veterinary drugs, and fertilizers
  • Acting as a key agent for international companies entering Ethiopia

5. Rang Real Estate

Rang Real Estate is committed to the development of high-quality mixed-use, residential, and commercial properties, contributing to:

  • Real estate solutions that align with Ethiopia's urban future
