Growing a Sustainable Future

About Us

Founded in 1996 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, RANGVet PLC is a pioneering, family-owned business with a bold vision: to transform Ethiopia’s agriculture and veterinary sectors by providing world-class products, expert guidance, and reliable services.

Established by a diverse and experienced team of veterinary specialists, agronomists, engineers, finance professionals, and business managers, RANGVet has grown from a single company into a group of businesses committed to advancing Ethiopia’s agricultural future.

With over two decades of consistent service, we continue to support farmers, agro-dealers, cooperatives, and institutions by delivering integrated solutions that improve productivity, sustainability, and livelihoods across the country.

Mission

To empower Ethiopian farmers and agro-professionals by delivering quality agricultural inputs, veterinary solutions, and equipment—supported by technical expertise and global innovation.

Vision

To become Ethiopia’s leading one-stop shop for agricultural and veterinary solutions, while building strong international partnerships and investing in local manufacturing and development.
